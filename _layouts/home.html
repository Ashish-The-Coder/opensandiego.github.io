We Are Open San Diego

We are a group of civic-minded San Diegans who meet regularly to learn how to best help citizens and local government have a more informed relationship with each other. Join the conversation and help if you can. We are also a Code For America brigade, and part of a national organization. Everyone is welcome at every skill level!

We meet monthly. Join Us!
